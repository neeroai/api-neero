## AI Capabilities: Deep Dive

### Salesforce Einstein Marketing

**Core Capabilities:**
- Campaign Optimizer (automate lifecycle)
- Segment creation with natural language
- Email content generation
- AI scoring for lead prioritization
- Send time optimization
- Predictive audience segmentation

**Einstein Bots (Conversational AI):**
- NLP for customer query comprehension
- Multi-channel: SMS, Chat, Slack, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp
- CRM data access
- Task automation: FAQs, case creation, scheduling

**Critical Limitations:**
1. **Vendor Lock-In:** Cannot use external AI models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google)
2. **WhatsApp Complexity:** 4-week phone approval, Einstein Bot setup requires expertise
3. **Add-On Pricing:** AI features require Advanced+ tiers
4. **Single Intelligence Layer:** All AI from Salesforce only
5. **Platform Dependency:** Requires extensive Salesforce ecosystem knowledge

**User Feedback:**
- "Gaps between Einstein's marketing promises and operational reality"
- Complex setup requires specialized Salesforce skills
- Cannot leverage best-of-breed AI models

### Bird AI Employees

**Core Capabilities:**
- **100,000+ businesses** using AI Employee platform
- Multi-LLM support: OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic (Claude), Vertex AI (Gemini), Groq
- Task-based architecture (not just chatbots)
- Knowledge Base auto-generation from websites
- Dynamic variables for personalization
- API integrations with business systems

**Key Advantages:**
1. **Model Flexibility:** Choose best AI per use case
   - GPT-4: Complex reasoning, conversational
   - Claude: Long context, analysis
   - Gemini: Multimodal (text + image)
   - Groq: Ultra-fast inference

2. **Cost Optimization:**
   - Use Gemini for cost-sensitive tasks (89% cheaper than Claude)
   - Use Groq for high-volume, low-latency needs
   - Mix models based on business requirements

3. **No Lock-In:**
   - Switch models as AI landscape evolves
   - Bring-your-own-model support
   - Not tied to single vendor's AI roadmap

4. **WhatsApp Native:**
   - No Einstein Bot complexity
   - Instant setup, no 4-week approval
   - Interactive messages built-in

**Strategic Advantage:**
> "Bird lets you choose the best AI for EACH task. Salesforce locks you into Einstein, whether it's the best model or not."

**Example Use Case:**
- **Sales conversations:** GPT-4 (best conversational AI)
- **Document analysis:** Claude (128K context window)
- **Image classification:** Gemini 2.0 (multimodal native)
- **High-volume routing:** Groq (ultra-fast, 90% cheaper)
